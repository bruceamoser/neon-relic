Anchor — Object & Memory

Section VII — Agent Biography
Before the Covenant — Who were you?
Former Spetsnaz sergeant, seconded to GRU special operations before defecting through Vienna in 1984. The artifact he found in an Afghan cave system killed three of his men and walked out of the mountain with him. The Covenant intercepted him at the Austrian border. He has not spoken Russian since.
