# People and Witness Photo Prompts

Use these prompts to generate late-1980s still photography for dossiers, witness boards, surveillance packets, and police files.

## Global guidance
- Buenos Aires, 1987 aesthetic
- analog film photography, realistic grain, period clothing, no modern tech
- avoid smartphones, modern cars, LED lighting, contemporary fashion branding
- framing should feel like police photos, private investigator stills, church archives, or tabloid telephoto shots

## Witness portrait
`1987 Buenos Aires witness photo, tired middle-aged civilian in a police holding room, analog flash photography, washed beige walls, slightly overexposed film, late Cold War fashion, documentary realism, no modern objects`

## Quiet detective source
`plainclothes Buenos Aires robbery detective, late 1980s municipal office, cigarette smoke haze, manila folders, green desk lamp, analog surveillance aesthetic, realistic film grain, subdued expression, no modern police equipment`

## Church archivist
`Catholic archivist nun in a restricted archive room, shelves of old ledgers and boxed documents, low tungsten light, late 1980s film still, realistic documentary style, no fantasy costuming`

## Compact handler surveillance still
`covert surveillance still of an elegant female black-market handler leaving a Buenos Aires hotel in 1987, long-lens film photography, slight blur, night street reflections, period car in background, no neon cyberpunk exaggeration`

## Wounded thief clinic photo
`forensic photo of a wounded thief in an illicit clinic, 1980s analog medical setting, improvised treatment room, unsettling but grounded realism, no gore excess, documentary flash photography`
