# Locations and Map Prompts

Use these prompts for location establishing art, tactical references, dossier inserts, and handout maps.

## Global guidance
- Buenos Aires, 1987
- analog realism over stylized genre art
- period signage, vehicles, architecture, interiors, and paper maps
- avoid satellite imagery, GIS overlays, modern street furniture, LED signage

## Estate aftermath exterior
`wealthy Recoleta estate exterior after a discreet high-value robbery, 1987 Buenos Aires, police tape minimal, old masonry facade, analog crime-scene photography, realistic and subdued`

## Hidden vault chamber
`old private vault chamber beneath an aristocratic estate, ritualized containment architecture disguised as a secure basement room, low practical lighting, realistic occult thriller production design, no fantasy dungeon elements`

## Church archive interior
`restricted Catholic archive room in Buenos Aires, shelves of ledgers, document boxes, dust, tungsten lamps, late-1980s realism, no modern compact shelving systems`

## Illicit clinic interior
`informal clandestine clinic room in 1987 Buenos Aires, improvised medical supplies, analog fluorescent lighting, documentary realism, tense but grounded`

## Temporary custody shrine
`abandoned chapel basement turned improvised relic shrine, 1980s South American urban environment, warped devotional staging, realistic physical props, no supernatural CGI`

## Case map handout
`top-down printed city district operations map for Buenos Aires 1987, paper street map style with hand-marked circles, arrows, notes, and color pins, suitable as a tabletop handout, no modern digital mapping aesthetics`
