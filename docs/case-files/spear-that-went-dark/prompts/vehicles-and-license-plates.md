# Vehicles and License Plate Prompts

Use these prompts for surveillance boards, vehicle ID sheets, checkpoint props, and stakeout inserts.

## Global guidance
- Argentina, 1987, grounded period vehicles
- no contemporary plate formats, no modern police light bars, no SUVs from later decades
- prefer sedans, compact cars, delivery vans, port vehicles, church cars, black-market courier cars

## Compact courier sedan
`1987 Buenos Aires surveillance photo of an inconspicuous full-size sedan used by black-market couriers, parked curbside at dusk, analog telephoto realism, late-1980s vehicle details only`

## Illicit clinic van
`weathered 1980s panel van outside a side-street clinic, documentary surveillance still, overcast light, realistic urban decay, no modern decals`

## Period license plate insert
`close-up prop photograph of late-1980s Argentine license plate mounted on a sedan, analog flash photo, realistic wear, slightly dirty bumper, no contemporary fonts or materials`

## Port cargo vehicle
`1987 port logistics truck in Buenos Aires cargo yard, analog industrial photography, muted colors, documentary realism, no container terminal modernity`

## Church sedan
`plain dark church-owned sedan outside a diocesan building at night, late-1980s film still, practical lighting, subtle atmosphere, no cinematic stylization`
