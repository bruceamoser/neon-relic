# Documents and Handout Prompts

Use these prompts for printed props, packet returns, archive excerpts, police paperwork, church memos, and tabloid clippings.

## Global guidance
- late-1980s paper stock, typewriter, dot-matrix, fax artifacts, photocopy noise, institutional stamps
- avoid modern UI, digital typography, clean contemporary layouts, QR codes, barcodes that feel post-1995

## Church custody memo
`1980s confidential Catholic custody memorandum, typed on aging cream paper, marginal annotations in blue ink, ecclesiastical stamp marks, slight photocopy degradation, realistic archival prop`

## Covenant archive packet
`restricted occult archive summary packet, 1987 intelligence dossier style, green-bar computer printout merged with typed annotations, stamped classification marks, manila file aesthetic, realistic paper texture`

## Police witness statement
`Buenos Aires police witness statement from 1987, typewritten form, handwritten corrections, coffee stain, case number, realistic municipal paperwork, analog photocopy texture`

## Tabloid clipping
`1987 tabloid newspaper clipping about blackout, robbery, and religious panic, cheap newsprint, bold sensational headline, halftone photo box, aged paper edges, realistic period layout`

## Thief notes
`panic-written criminal notes on wrinkled notebook paper, ballpoint pen, frantic handwriting, blood smear optional but restrained, late 1980s found document prop, realistic physical paper scan`
