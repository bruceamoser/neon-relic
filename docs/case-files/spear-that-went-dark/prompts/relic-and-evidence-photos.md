# Relic and Evidence Photo Prompts

Use these prompts for evidence boards, archive inserts, close-up relic references, and surveillance stills tied to the Spear.

## Global guidance
- treat the Spear as physically real, historically dense, and dangerous
- avoid fantasy glow, game-asset stylization, exaggerated holy visual effects
- late-1980s evidence photography, archive photography, or field documentation style

## Spear evidence close-up
`forensic evidence photograph of an ancient spearhead and restored shaft assembly believed to be the Spear of Destiny, late-1980s analog documentation style, neutral backdrop, realistic patina, unsettling gravitas, no magical glow`

## Wrapped transport state
`evidence photo of a dangerous relic wrapped in lead-lined cloth, ash and salt residue visible, practical containment materials, analog flash photography, realistic field documentation`

## Estate vault residue
`crime-scene close-up of a breached containment cradle in a private vault, cloth torn, metallic residue, devotional traces mixed with security hardware, 1980s forensic photo realism`

## Surveillance shrine still
`grainy surveillance still from an improvised shrine where an ancient spear relic is being kept, low light, analog CCTV feel, unstable image, realistic and disturbing, no overt supernatural special effects`

## Wound pattern reference
`forensic reference board showing repeated cruciform wound pattern sketches and medical photo annotations, 1980s investigative layout, realistic and restrained, institutional documentation style`
