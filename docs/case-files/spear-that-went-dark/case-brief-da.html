I — Mystery Statement
Summarize the entire case in one sentence.
A Tier 4 sovereignty relic has surfaced in Buenos Aires after a professional theft crew funded by Vantablack Compact cutouts breached a private custody chamber. If left uncontained, the Spear will select a bearer and trigger a public mandate event. Meanwhile the Compact, the Church, local police, and foreign buyers are all moving on the same broken chain of custody. The agents need the old containment protocol before they can recover it safely.
II — The Real Situation
What is actually happening? Who is really involved? How does the case resolve?
The Spear of Destiny was held for decades in a private vault beneath the Ferreyra estate under a postwar custody arrangement with the Church. The vault was not a safe — it was a ritual containment site maintained through a liturgical suppression sequence. Three nights ago, a professional theft crew breached the vault, broke the containment lineage, and failed to complete the scheduled buyer handoff. The Spear is now hidden somewhere in Buenos Aires, active and escalating. The heist crew has fractured, a surviving thief is suffering wound bloom, a Compact fixer is becoming a bearer candidate, and the Church is quietly running a parallel recovery. The agents must locate the Spear, learn its containment truths, and re-establish custody before the artifact selects a claimant and the city becomes the ritual site.
III — Agent Objectives
Primary Objective
Locate the Spear, learn the containment truths, and re-establish custody without triggering an urban threshold event.
Secondary Objective
Prevent export. Identify and neutralize the emerging bearer. Preserve operational secrecy.
IV — Containment Truths Summary
Trigger — What makes the artifact more active or dangerous?
The Spear becomes more active when moved repeatedly between custodians, exposed to fresh blood, or carried by someone making claims of destiny, divine mandate, or holy violence.
Appetite — What does the artifact feed on, amplify, or attract?
Wound trauma, ideological certainty, righteous violence, proximity to a self-declared chosen bearer, and speech that turns command into sacred obligation.
Quiescence — What conditions make it safe to handle or contain?
Substitute custody sequence: lead-lined cloth over consecrated ash and salt, isolation from followers and cameras, silence (no claimant language), low light, and a Keep-led suppression rite before transport.
V — Key Actors & Factions
Who are the principal NPCs and organizations?
Lucia Ferreyra — estate custodian, knows partial containment sequence. Detective Mateo Suárez — robbery detective, quiet source. Father Esteban Vale — Church black-chamber intermediary. Sister Inés Barral — Church archivist, best containment source. Ramiro “Rami” Acosta — surviving thief, wound bloom. Sabine Voss — Compact regional handler. Iván Luján — Compact fixer, strongest bearer candidate. Brother Tomás Arce — false ecclesiastical broker.
VI — Resolution & Endgame
Best Case
Agents learn all containment truths, identify the bearer, and execute a controlled recovery at the Hidden Shrine using the full Quiescence protocol. Spear enters Covenant custody. Export prevented.
Worst Case
The Spear selects a bearer who reaches full activation. Public miracle/atrocity event. Mass-casualty zone. Long-term secrecy lost. Multiple factions converge on the artifact simultaneously.

VIII — DA Notes
Pacing cues, tone reminders, things to watch for
The Spear is simultaneously sacred relic, imperial mandate, and weaponizable asset — each faction understands only one slice. Play the bearer candidate as tragic before formidable. The Spear does not make soldiers — it makes prophets who believe their own myth. Resist the urge to reveal the Spear early. Let the agents assemble the picture from institutional fragments and witness contradictions. The case works best when the players feel the timeline pressure without being told the countdown numbers. Cross-links between organizations should feel like emergent consequences, not scripted plot beats.
