# PRINTABLE PLAY AID — NPC Dossiers

Use this sheet for quick table reference. Read down the "Public face" line first when introducing the NPC, then hold the "Secret" and "Use at table" lines for DA guidance.

## Lucia Ferreyra

- Role: estate retainer and last credible witness to the old custody procedure
- Public face: exhausted, proud, private, furious that outsiders opened the vault
- Secret: knows parts of the silence protocol and the family's postwar arrangement
- Use at table: cautious ally, source of old-custody detail, moral witness to the estate cover story

## Detective Mateo Suarez

- Role: robbery detective on the edge of a much stranger case
- Public face: competent, skeptical, under pressure from superiors
- Secret: knows witness contradictions are being quietly buried
- Use at table: quiet source, pressure valve, possible collateral if police front spikes

## Father Esteban Vale

- Role: black-chamber Church intermediary
- Public face: measured, courteous, conservative, harder than he seems
- Secret: wants reclaiming the relic more than telling the truth about prior Church compromise
- Use at table: rival custodian, partial ally, source of doctrinal warnings

## Sister Ines Barral

- Role: archivist and ritual conservator
- Public face: exacting, calm, observant
- Secret: believes the Church no longer deserves sole custody
- Use at table: best source of liturgical containment detail if trust is earned

## Ramiro Acosta

- Role: surviving thief
- Public face: dying, terrified, intermittently euphoric
- Secret: heard the handoff collapse in real time and knows who started speaking like a claimant
- Use at table: broken witness, urgency node, bearer clue source

## Sabine Voss

- Role: Compact regional handler
- Public face: controlled, elegant, transactional
- Secret: is trying to recover the sale narrative before buyers realize she lost control
- Use at table: mover of the Compact front, architect of quiet pressure

## Ivan Lujan

- Role: fixer, courier, possible bearer
- Public face: practical operator with nationalist ties
- Secret: already half-convinced the Spear has validated him
- Use at table: bearer candidate, escalation hinge, tragedy point

## Brother Tomas Arce

- Role: ecclesiastical broker and information parasite
- Public face: charming, useful, slippery
- Secret: sells fragments to anyone who sounds certain enough
- Use at table: unreliable access point, false lead risk, contact unlock

## Quick Sorting Guide

- Best sources of custodial truth: Lucia Ferreyra, Sister Ines Barral
- Best sources of police or public-pressure truth: Detective Mateo Suarez
- Best sources of failed-handoff truth: Ramiro Acosta, Brother Tomas Arce
- Most likely active movers of rival pressure: Sabine Voss, Ivan Lujan, Father Esteban Vale
