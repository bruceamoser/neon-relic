# PRINTABLE PLAY AID — Case Board Tracker

Track the case as narrative pressure rather than a linear sequence.

Use this sheet behind the screen or as a shared table worksheet. Update it at the end of every shift, especially after packet returns, public incidents, or any scene that worsens a front.

## Quick use

- write current values in pencil or erasable marker
- note visible signs separately from hidden DA-side thresholds
- mark scene availability the moment a clue, contact, or time window opens it
- do not reveal dormant fronts to players until they have fiction on the table
