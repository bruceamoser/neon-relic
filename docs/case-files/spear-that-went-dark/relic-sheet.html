Activation & Effect
Activation Condition — What triggers the artifact?
Blood exposure, public claim-language, and contested authority converge around it. A carrier invoking mandate, sacrifice, divine sanction, victory, kingship, empire, or national rebirth around fresh blood or a public audience.
Mechanical Effect — What does it do when activated?
Force ideological submission or ecstatic obedience in a zone; reveal/intensify the nearest claimant dynamic in a crowd; transform a confrontation into a legitimacy crisis; inflict catastrophic Corruption on anyone denying its authority in proximity.
Fracture — What happens on an Artifact Die roll of 1?
A public miracle/atrocity manifests (advance relic milestones); the current bearer suffers wound bloom, conviction collapse, or ecstatic command-delusion; nearby witnesses treat random violence as sacred proof; electronics fail in a widening radius and the scene becomes impossible to contain quietly.
Containment Profile
Full handling and transport requirements
Wrap in lead-lined cloth over consecrated ash and salt. Isolate bearer from followers, witnesses, and cameras. No one may address it as relic, lance, spear, destiny, empire, victory, or instrument of God during handling. Transfer in silence under low light. Keep-led suppression rite before vehicle transport. All active claimant language shut down in containment zone. If conditions are broken, fill 1 extra shift quadrant and/or worsen one related organization.
