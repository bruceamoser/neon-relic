# Image Prompt — Handout 7: Case Evidence Montage

Copy the entire prompt below into an image generator. Output filename: `spear-handout-7-case-evidence-montage.png`

---

Create a single full-page image of a late-1980s Buenos Aires investigation evidence montage arranged as a clean, organized case-support page rather than a chaotic conspiracy board. The layout should present four to five distinct visual elements on a neutral surface as though assembled for an investigator's briefing binder: one analog surveillance still of a figure on a Buenos Aires street, shot with grainy film and a telephoto compression look; one section of a paper city map of Buenos Aires with hand-drawn route markings in pen or highlighter showing movement between two or three locations; one evidence photograph of an object resembling a historical spearhead or lance fragment, photographed under flat institutional lighting on an evidence table; one image of a period vehicle — a late-1970s or early-1980s sedan, van, or cargo truck — parked on a Buenos Aires side street; and one small forensic reference element such as an evidence tag, a wound sketch on lined paper, or a case-number label. Arrange these elements with subtle borders, light overlap, and archival tape or paperclip details so the page reads as curated investigative support material. Use grounded 1980s realism throughout: paper maps instead of digital GIS, surveillance-camera framing instead of action-movie composition, analog film grain, and physically real evidence rather than fantasy spectacle. No red-string conspiracy-board clichés, no modern street details, no glossy magazine montage, no game-card framing, no cyberpunk UI, no CGI effects, no glowing occult elements, no supernatural lighting. The finished image should feel like the final support page in a high-quality tabletop RPG case packet.
