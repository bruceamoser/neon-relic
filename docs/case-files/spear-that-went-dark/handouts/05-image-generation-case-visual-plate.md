## Core visual goal

Build one coherent case-visual montage that feels like assembled support material from an investigation binder. It should contain enough variety to imply witnesses, movement routes, and relic evidence without looking like a chaotic conspiracy board.

## Desired image contents

Include a balanced mix of the following kinds of material:

- one or two witness or surveillance stills with analog film grain
- one route, map, or vehicle insert suggesting movement through Buenos Aires
- one relic or evidence photo tied directly to the Spear

## Scene and object guidance

Use late-1980s Buenos Aires realism throughout:

- period sedans, clinic van, church sedan, or cargo vehicle
- restrained surveillance framing, not action-movie composition
- relic evidence should look historically dense and dangerous but physically real
- maps should feel like paper city maps with hand marks, not digital GIS products

## Composition guidance

- arrange the plate like curated evidence support, not a meme wall or detective-board parody
- keep image borders, overlap, and labels subtle
- the page should feel printable and useful in a rulebook appendix
- avoid overpacking too many miniature panels; clarity matters more than quantity

`Late-1980s Buenos Aires occult investigation visual plate, assembled like a clean evidence-support page for a tabletop RPG: analog surveillance stills of witnesses or suspects, a route or city-map insert with hand markings, period vehicle or transit imagery, a realistic relic evidence photograph tied to the Spear of Destiny, and restrained forensic reference material, all presented with believable archival and investigative texture, no modern digital UI, no cyberpunk styling, no supernatural glow, suitable for a printed rulebook appendix`
