# Image Prompt — Location 2: Police Station

Copy the entire prompt below into an image generator. Output filename: `spear-location-2-police-station.png`

---

Create a single full-page portrait image of a Buenos Aires police precinct interior in the late 1980s, viewed from the perspective of someone entering the robbery division. The space is a large open-plan bullpen with metal desks cluttered with papers, overflowing ashtrays, rotary telephones, and manual typewriters. Fluorescent tube lighting casts a flat greenish-white wash over everything. A corkboard on one wall is pinned with crime-scene photos, witness sketches, and handwritten notes connected by string. Several detectives in shirtsleeves work at their desks or stand in small groups holding coffee cups and folders. A glass-partitioned interview room is visible in the background with its blinds half-drawn. The floor is scuffed linoleum, the ceiling tiles are stained, and a wall-mounted clock reads mid-afternoon. The atmosphere should feel overworked, institutional, and slightly oppressive — a place where cases pile up faster than they close. Use a late-1980s analog photography aesthetic: flat fluorescent lighting, muted institutional colors, visible grain, no digital crispness. No fantasy elements, no supernatural imagery, no modern computers or equipment, no Hollywood-police-drama styling.
