# Recovered Thief Notes

Not a sale. Not after the box opened.

L. said hold it like cargo. Wrong. Wrong the second Raul touched the cloth and started talking like somebody was listening from the old wars.

No one would shut up after that. Everybody had a reason why it had to be theirs, why we were the ones who got through, why the route changed, why the buyer would understand.

Buyer man would not touch it. Kept asking for proof. Kept asking if it was stable. Raul laughed at him and said stable is for governments and dead men.

Lights went. Cars died. Marta said put it back. Hector hit her. Said history had picked us. Said there are moments where command returns.

We split after the clinic stop. I don't know who has it now. I know whoever does is hearing the same thing: you were not chosen to carry it. You were chosen to prove you could.
