# Image Prompt — Location 4: Currency Exchange

Copy the entire prompt below into an image generator. Output filename: `spear-location-4-currency-exchange.png`

---

Create a single full-page portrait image of a small illicit currency exchange and money-transfer office in a Buenos Aires commercial district, used as a front for laundering antiquities-theft proceeds. The space is cramped and functional — a reinforced counter with a scratched plexiglass partition, a wall of wire-caged safe-deposit cubbies, a ceiling fan turning slowly, and a back room visible through a beaded curtain or half-open door. On the counter sit a mechanical adding machine, stacks of carbon-copy receipt pads, a rotary telephone, and a small television tuned to static or a news broadcast. Hand-lettered signs in Spanish advertise exchange rates. The lighting is a mix of buzzing fluorescent tubes and warm daylight filtering through a grimy street-level window covered with iron bars. The atmosphere should feel transactional, layered, and faintly menacing — this is where clean money becomes untraceable and questions are not welcome. Use a late-1980s analog photography aesthetic: mixed lighting temperatures, muted tones, visible grain, slight overexposure from the window. No fantasy elements, no supernatural imagery, no modern equipment, no digital styling, no cinematic drama.
