# Image Prompt — NPC 2: Detective Mateo Suárez

Copy the entire prompt below into an image generator. Output filename: `spear-npc-2-detective-mateo-suarez.png`

---

Create a single portrait-oriented character illustration of Detective Mateo Suárez, a Buenos Aires robbery detective in his early forties. He is competent, skeptical, and visibly under pressure from superiors. His face is square-jawed with a dark mustache, tired eyes, and the slightly rumpled look of a man working double shifts. He wears a loosened tie over a wrinkled dress shirt with rolled-up sleeves, a shoulder holster visible under an open sport coat. He holds a manila case folder in one hand or has it tucked under his arm. The background should suggest a busy Argentine police precinct in the late 1980s — fluorescent overhead lighting, a cluttered desk edge, a corkboard with pinned photos and notes visible behind him. His expression is wary and measuring, as though he suspects the person across from him knows more than they are saying. The overall tone should be realistic and gritty — this is a working detective in a city where cases go sideways. Use a late-1980s analog portrait aesthetic: slightly warm fluorescent cast, muted tones, faint grain. No fantasy elements, no glowing effects, no supernatural imagery, no modern styling, no dramatic action poses.
