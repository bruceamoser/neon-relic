# Image Prompt — Location 6: Port / Export Route

Copy the entire prompt below into an image generator. Output filename: `spear-location-6-port-export-route.png`

---

Create a single full-page portrait image of a section of the Buenos Aires port district at night, showing a cargo staging area used for illicit export. The scene includes a concrete wharf edge with bollards and heavy mooring chains, a row of corrugated-metal shipping containers stacked two high, a parked panel van or cargo truck with its rear doors open revealing wooden crates and packing material, and a small dockside office or guard shack with a single lit window. A gantry crane or loading arm is silhouetted against the night sky. The lighting is industrial — amber sodium vapor lamps casting long orange shadows across wet pavement, with pockets of deep darkness between containers. The Rio de la Plata is visible as a flat dark expanse beyond the wharf. The atmosphere should feel operational, exposed, and tense — a place where cargo moves without questions between midnight and dawn. Use a late-1980s analog photography aesthetic: sodium-vapor orange cast, deep blacks, visible grain, slight lens flare from the dock lights. No fantasy elements, no supernatural imagery, no action sequences, no modern container-terminal automation, no digital styling.
