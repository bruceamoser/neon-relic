# Image Prompt — Location 5: Illicit Clinic

Copy the entire prompt below into an image generator. Output filename: `spear-location-5-illicit-clinic.png`

---

Create a single full-page portrait image of a back-alley unlicensed clinic in a Buenos Aires working-class barrio, used to treat criminals and people who cannot go to hospitals. The space is a converted ground-floor apartment or basement — bare concrete walls with peeling paint, a single operating-style table covered with a stained sheet, a rolling metal cart holding surgical instruments and mismatched bottles, an IV stand improvised from a coat rack, and a bare overhead bulb casting harsh downward light. A curtain on a wire divides the treatment area from a narrow cot where a patient might recover. On a side table sit a transistor radio, a half-empty bottle of cheap alcohol, cotton wadding, and a pack of cigarettes. The floor is cracked tile with a drain. The atmosphere should feel desperate, functional, and clandestine — a place that exists because the alternative is bleeding out in a gutter. Use a late-1980s analog photography aesthetic: harsh single-source overhead light, deep corner shadows, desaturated palette, visible grain, claustrophobic framing. No fantasy elements, no supernatural imagery, no visible gore or blood, no modern medical equipment, no digital styling, no horror-movie atmosphere.
