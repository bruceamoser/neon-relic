Create a single full-page portrait image of a late-1980s restricted internal archive summary document from a secretive investigative society called the Verdant Covenant. The heading block should include a typed subject line referencing a relic custody record, a classification stamp reading RESTRICTED or EYES ONLY, a date field circa 1987, and a file reference number. The body text should be dense typewriter output with slight ribbon inconsistency, covering a brief custodial history of the Spear of Destiny: centuries of Church guardianship, the relic's removal during wartime, fragmented postwar custody, and a closing note flagging the object as high-priority for recovery. Include one or two marginal annotations in blue or black ink — a handwritten note, an underline, or a bracket — as though a handler reviewed the page before filing. Add subtle period wear: a single fold crease, faint discoloration along one edge, and maybe a light coffee ring or thumbprint shadow, but keep the page fully readable. The overall tone should be bureaucratic, sober, and institutional — this is internal paperwork, not a movie prop. Use 1987 analog office realism throughout. No modern fonts, no laser-print crispness, no digital design elements, no fantasy parchment, no glowing symbols, no supernatural effects, no glossy layout, no color printing.
