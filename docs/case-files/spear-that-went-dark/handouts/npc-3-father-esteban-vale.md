# Image Prompt — NPC 3: Father Esteban Vale

Create a single portrait-oriented character illustration of Father Esteban Vale, a Catholic Church black-chamber intermediary in his mid-fifties. He is measured, courteous, conservative, and harder than he appears. His face is cleanly shaven with sharp cheekbones, deep-set intelligent eyes, and an expression of careful neutrality that conceals calculation. He wears a black clerical shirt with a Roman collar, a dark overcoat or suit jacket over it, and carries himself with the quiet authority of a man accustomed to institutional power. One hand rests on a small leather document case or breviary. The background should suggest a dim church office or sacristy — dark wood furniture, a crucifix on the wall partially visible, a heavy door ajar behind him, warm low lamplight. The overall tone should be realistic and restrained — this is a man who recovers things the Church does not want discussed publicly. Use a late-1980s analog portrait aesthetic: warm shadows, muted color palette, slight grain, painterly realism. No fantasy elements, no glowing halos, no supernatural imagery, no modern styling, no horror tropes.
