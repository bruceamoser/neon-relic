= Sample Case File: The Spear That Went Dark

This chapter presents a full sample case built for the revised Neon Relic case structure: an Operations Board with organization rows and relic milestones, Locations with NPC Cards and Information Cards, delayed information returns, and a relic whose containment problem matters as much as its location.

Use it as both a playable operation and a model for authoring future cases.

== Working Premise

The *Spear of Destiny* -- or a relic credibly believed to be that spearhead and its restored shaft assembly -- was held in a private vault beneath a fortified estate in Buenos Aires under discreet aristocratic custody. Three nights ago, the estate was hit by a professional theft crew funded through Vantablack Compact cutouts. The operation succeeded just long enough to become dangerous.

The theft team breached the estate, exposed the relic to a broken custody sequence, and failed to complete the scheduled handoff. Since then, the Spear has gone still somewhere in the city while hospitals, thieves, clergy, police, and black-market intermediaries all react to different pieces of the same event.

The agents arrive after the clean version of the crime is already over. What remains is a contaminated intelligence market circling an artifact that behaves like:

* a Christian wound relic
* an imperial mandate object
* a Cold War occult weapon

No faction understands all three at once.

== Agent Objectives

The team must:

. Locate the Spear before the Compact or foreign buyers recover its chain.
. Determine what interrupted the handoff.
. Learn the Spear's containment truths before direct recovery.
. Prevent export.
. Re-establish custody without triggering an urban threshold event.

== The Spear of Destiny

|===
| Tier | 4
| Category | Relic / Instrument / War Crown Surrogate
| Risk Tag | Catastrophic
| Surface Read | A Christian relic of incalculable symbolic weight
| Operational Read | A sovereignty amplifier and metaphysical conflict engine

| Corruption Cost | +3 Corruption on activation

| Emission | Pulse becoming Burst as relic milestones advance on the Operations Board
|===

=== What the Spear Does

The Spear does not merely wound. It *authorizes*.

Exposure produces escalating conviction that bloodshed, legitimacy, and command have fused into one sacred right. Carriers and witnesses may begin to believe:

* they were chosen to bear history
* command belongs to whoever can endure the wound-object longest
* violence is righteous if framed as restoration
* old crusades, empires, and states were interrupted rather than ended

=== Activation Condition

The Spear activates when blood exposure, public claim-language, and contested authority converge around it. A single trigger may not be enough. A carrier invoking mandate, sacrifice, divine sanction, victory, kingship, empire, or national rebirth around fresh blood or a public audience is usually sufficient.

=== Mechanical Effect

When fully activated, the Spear can do one of the following, depending on scene framing:

* force immediate ideological submission or ecstatic obedience in a zone
* reveal and intensify the nearest claimant dynamic in a crowd
* transform a confrontation into a legitimacy crisis, making all bystanders choose a side
* inflict catastrophic Corruption on anyone trying to deny its symbolic authority while in direct proximity

The Spear should not be framed as a weapon with a simple attack profile. It changes the meaning of a scene before it changes bodies.

=== Fracture

On an Artifact Die roll of `1`, one of the following occurs:

* a public miracle / atrocity manifests — fill 1 extra shift quadrant on the phases row (advancing relic milestones)
* the current bearer suffers wound bloom, conviction collapse, or ecstatic command-delusion
* nearby witnesses begin treating random violence as sacred proof
* electronics fail in a widening radius and the scene becomes impossible to contain quietly

=== Containment Profile

The Spear can be moved safely only if all of the following are true:

* it is wrapped in lead-lined cloth over consecrated ash and salt
* the current bearer is isolated from followers, witnesses, and cameras
* no one addresses it as relic, lance, spear, destiny, empire, victory, or instrument of God during handling
* transfer occurs in silence under low light
* a Keep-led suppression rite is completed before vehicle transport
* active claimant language is shut down in the containment zone

If these conditions are broken, transport is still possible -- but the DA should fill 1 extra shift quadrant on the phases row, worsen one related organization, or both.

== Operations Board

The Operations Board for this case uses 14 columns (14 → 1, days until catastrophe). Six organization rows track external pressure. Relic milestones are keyed to the phases row.

=== Phases Row — Relic Milestones (Lance Threshold Build)

This is what the Spear itself does if no one re-establishes custody. Milestones trigger when the DA fills the shift quadrant that crosses the phase number.

* *Phase 44:* Local hospitals and street reports begin showing recognizable cruciform-wound patterns.
* *Phase 32:* Handlers suffer reopened wounds, religious certainty, and dream-imprint episodes. Advance O3 (Local Police Inquiry) by 1 square.
* *Phase 20:* Radios, surveillance gear, and vehicles fail near the Spear; witnesses report that "someone chosen is coming." O6 (Media Exposure) activates if still dormant.
* *Phase 8:* The Spear begins selecting a claimant. Advance O5 (Foreign Buyer Window) by 2 squares if active.
* *Phase 1:* Catastrophe — public miracle/atrocity, mass-casualty zone, or violent claimant manifestation.

=== Organization Rows

==== O1: Vantablack Compact Recovery Operation — Active (starting value 9)

* *O1M1 (column 7):* Bribery channels activate across police, hospital, and customs routes. Advance O3 (Local Police Inquiry) by 1 square.
* *O1M2 (column 4):* Field professionals replace cutouts; disappearances and abductions begin. Advance O2 (Heist Crew Fracture) by 1 square.
* *O1M3 (column 1):* The Compact has a likely district, safehouse, or export corridor.
* *O1M4 (column 0):* The Compact secures the Spear or launches its final snatch. O5 (Foreign Buyer Window) activates if still dormant.

==== O2: Heist Crew Fracture — Active (starting value 6)

* *O2M1 (column 5):* One thief disappears with money and papers.
* *O2M2 (column 3):* A wounded thief surfaces through a clinic, fence, confessional chain, or lover. Advance O3 (Local Police Inquiry) by 2 squares and O1 (Compact Recovery) by 1 square.
* *O2M3 (column 1):* The surviving crew attempts desperate relocation. Advance Lance Threshold Build — fill 1 extra shift quadrant on the phases row.

==== O3: Local Police Inquiry — Active (starting value 10)

* *O3M1 (column 8):* Robbery detectives begin consolidating witness contradictions and unexplained injuries.
* *O3M2 (column 6):* A protected witness is released, moved, or mishandled. Advance O1 (Compact Recovery) by 2 squares and O4 (Church Custody Cell) by 1 square.
* *O3M3 (column 3):* Checkpoints, raids, and formal pressure begin closing the city. O6 (Media Exposure) activates if still dormant.
* *O3M4 (column 0):* Police intervention lands directly on the live artifact scene. Advance Lance Threshold Build — fill 1 extra shift quadrant on the phases row.

==== O4: Church Custody Cell — Active (starting value 12)

* *O4M1 (column 9):* Quiet observers begin monitoring hospitals, morgues, and aristocratic channels.
* *O4M2 (column 6):* Church observers identify Compact routes and begin tailing them. Advance O1 (Compact Recovery) by 1 square.
* *O4M3 (column 3):* A retrieval cleric / black-chamber custodian arrives with custody demands and incomplete ritual knowledge. Advance O3 (Local Police Inquiry) by 1 square if Church influence is used to divert attention.

==== O5: Foreign Buyer Window — Dormant (starting value 8)

Activates when the Compact regains custody, proof of custody reaches buyers, or a viable export corridor is secured.

* *O5M1 (column 5):* Advance funds move and courier infrastructure appears.
* *O5M2 (column 2):* Diplomatic pouch, private flight, or maritime handoff is imminent.
* *O5M3 (column 0):* The Spear leaves Argentine control.

==== O6: Media Exposure — Dormant (starting value 6)

Activates when public effects, police noise, hospital leaks, or visible violence make secrecy impossible.

* *O6M1 (column 3):* Tabloids and radio link the heist to blackouts, wounds, and "holy signs."
* *O6M2 (column 0):* The public story locks in and long-term secrecy is lost.

== Critical Revelations

These truths must remain reachable through multiple paths.

=== Trigger

The Spear becomes more active when:

* moved repeatedly between custodians
* exposed to fresh blood
* carried by someone making claims of destiny, divine mandate, kingship, national rebirth, or holy violence

=== Appetite

It feeds on:

* wound trauma
* ideological certainty
* righteous violence
* proximity to a self-declared chosen bearer
* speech that turns command into sacred obligation

=== Tether

The current tether is not a single person. It is a broken chain of custody. The estate vault's containment lineage descended through wartime seizure, clandestine relocation, and postwar black custody. The theft crew broke the ritual sequence that kept the Spear dormant.

=== Quiescence

The Spear can be moved only under a substitute custody sequence: shielding, silence, no claimant language, and a Keep-led suppression rite.

=== Fracture Logic

If agents attempt brute-force recovery without quiescence, the Spear does not merely resist. It turns the retrieval into a public contest of legitimacy.

== Packet Handouts and Player-Facing Documents

The following handouts are written for direct use as packet returns, case props, interview excerpts, or player-facing inserts. Present them selectively rather than all at once. === Handout 1: Covenant Archive Summary

Use this as the cleanest internal brief available to the team after a Wayfinder or Keep packet request. It should make the agents more cautious, not more confident.

[quote]
____
*Covenant Archive Summary*

*Subject:* Potential Longinus-class relic in black-custody circulation, Buenos Aires channel, postwar transfer lineage compromised.

*Archive Classification:* Keep / Catalogers / Restricted Custodial Sequence Material

*Working Assessment:* The object locally described as the Spear should not be treated as a devotional relic alone. Prior Covenant notes classify this family of objects as mandate relics: items that bind wound trauma to political legitimacy. Their danger lies less in simple curse behavior and more in their ability to convert belief, blood, and command into one self-reinforcing loop.

*Known Historical Pattern:*

* relic surfaces near periods of political fracture or succession anxiety
* carriers quickly develop conviction that history requires violent completion
* witnesses rationalize surrounding violence as justified, fated, or sacred
* custody breaks are more dangerous than long-term sealed storage

*Handling Notes:*

* do not permit public language of destiny, triumph, empire, holy rule, or chosen bloodline around the object
* isolate any current carrier from followers, cameras, and symbolic staging
* establish substitute custody protocol before transport
* expect ideological contagion before visible paranormal effects

*Archive Warning:* This item class does not merely corrupt. It authorizes. Field teams that approach it as contraband rather than claim-object have historically lost control of the human terrain first.
____

=== Handout 2: Church Custody Memorandum

Use this through Church contact access, black-chamber archives, or a partial packet routed through a sympathetic custodian. It should deepen the sense that prior containment existed for reasons no one wants to admit publicly.

[quote]
____
*Church Custody Memorandum*

*For Restricted Circulation:* Custodial review regarding the lance relic currently associated with the Buenos Aires family vault.

The family in question should not be described as owners. They were temporary lay custodians under an arrangement established after wartime displacement and never publicly regularized. The arrangement was tolerated because open acknowledgment would have created political, doctrinal, and diplomatic consequences no authority wished to inherit.

The object has always presented three linked dangers:

* scandal of authenticity
* political misuse
* devotional catastrophe

Past custodians reported that direct handling without procedural silence produced immediate changes in rhetoric among nearby witnesses. Speech patterns shifted first. Men spoke as if command had returned to them. Existing grievances became providential.

The older black-chamber recommendation was clear:

* no crowds
* no acclamation
* no blood near the object
* no use of triumphal or imperial titles in the containment zone
* no transfer without liturgical sequence and material shielding

If the sequence has been broken, reclamation by force alone is contraindicated. A broken chain of custody may inflame the object rather than secure it.
____

=== Handout 3: Museum and Academic Abstract

Use this through museum, university, archive, or public-record research. It is valuable because it sounds mundane while quietly confirming that the relic is dangerous even if treated as mere political myth.

[quote]
____
*Museum and Academic Abstract*

*Title:* Imperial Relic Traditions and the Political Afterlife of the Holy Lance

*Abstract:* This paper surveys the long political history of lance relic traditions associated with the Passion cycle, with particular attention to the recurring claim that possession of a specific spearhead confers or symbolizes legitimate rule. While modern scholarship treats many of these traditions as layered medieval constructions, the social effect of the belief has been persistent and measurable.

Across late antique, medieval, and modern contexts, the lance functions less as a devotional object than as a sovereignty token. It appears in coronation narratives, military propaganda, saintly myth, and authoritarian pageantry. The relic's cultural power lies in its instability: it can be framed as sacred witness, military inheritance, or proof that violence has been sanctified.

*Research Note:* Any surviving object linked to this tradition would be culturally volatile even if entirely mundane. In a charged political environment, claims surrounding it could inspire cult devotion, nationalist appropriation, apocalyptic interpretation, or prestige-market competition.

*Curatorial Conclusion:* The artifact's meaning would likely shift depending on who controls the narrative around it. That instability may be more dangerous than authenticity itself.
____

=== Handout 4: Black-Market Intelligence Brief

Use this as Counterintelligence output, stolen broker notes, or a packet request routed through illicit antiquities channels. It should clarify the buyer ecology without making the market feel orderly.

[quote]
____
*Black-Market Intelligence Brief*

*Distribution:* Counterintelligence / Recovery / Need-to-Know

*Subject:* Buyer interest cluster surrounding lance-class Christian war relic in Southern Cone transit channels.

*Executive Summary:* The object is attractive to at least three buyer types:

* prestige collectors seeking rarity and deniability
* militant networks seeking symbolic legitimacy and propaganda value
* intelligence intermediaries interested in coercive, ritual, or psychological applications

*Why the Market Cares:* The relic is valuable even before authenticity is settled. Competing myths increase value. The most committed buyers do not require museum-grade proof. They require a plausible chain, symbolic usability, and enough fear around the object to make rivals hesitate.

*Current Market Behavior:*

* the Compact is spending aggressively to contain embarrassment and recover control of the sale narrative
* secondary brokers are asking for proof-of-custody, not scholarly verification
* at least one foreign network is willing to accept the object if staged as a movement relic rather than a devotional artifact

*Operational Warning:* If photographs circulate showing the relic in a charged ritual, military, or nationalist context, the sale window will narrow and intensify. After that point, recovery becomes less about interception and more about interrupting a myth in motion.

== DA Quick Summary

*What happened?* The Compact funded the theft. The crew breached the estate. The Spear destabilized the transfer. The handoff failed. The relic is now hidden, active, and pulling multiple factions toward one contaminated custody crisis.

*What do the agents need?* Where the crew broke apart, who now has proximity control, what the old containment procedure was, and how to move the relic without completing its escalation.

*What makes the case work?* The object is simultaneously sacred relic, imperial mandate, and weaponizable asset. Each faction understands only one slice of that truth, and the agents must assemble all three before the city becomes the ritual site.
